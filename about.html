How it began

From a garage idea
to 500 cities

MiniShop started in 2020 when our founder Rajan Mehta noticed a simple problem: buying quality electronics or fashion online meant wading through unreliable sellers, inflated prices, and zero after-sale support.

We set out to change that — building a platform where every listing is verified, every price is honest, and every customer is treated like family. Today we're a team of 80+ people delivering across 500+ cities.

Our promise: the same product you'd find in a premium store, delivered to your door at the right price, backed by real human support.

What drives us

Our Values

Every decision we make comes back to three principles we set on day one.

Trust First

Every product is quality-checked. Every seller is verified. We never list something we wouldn't buy for our own families.

Fair Pricing

No hidden fees, no price manipulation. We show you the market price and our price — you always know you're getting a fair deal.

Real Support

Real humans, not bots. Our support team is available 7 days a week by phone, chat, and email — and they actually solve problems.

Sustainable Packaging

100% recyclable packaging across all orders by 2026. We're reducing plastic in every shipment we send out.

Secure Checkout

Bank-grade encryption on every transaction. We never store card details and always offer 2FA on accounts.

Hassle-free Returns

Don't love it? Return it within 7 days — no questions asked, refund processed within 48 hours.
